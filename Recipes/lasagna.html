Layered Lasagna

Description

Indulge in layers of savory satisfaction with our classic lasagna recipe. Crafted with tender pasta, rich marinara sauce, creamy cheese, and flavorful ground meat, each bite promises a symphony of flavors. Perfect for gatherings or cozy family dinners, our lasagna recipe is a timeless favorite that will leave everyone craving seconds. Dive into comfort and culinary delight with this hearty Italian masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 jar (24-46 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1 pound grounded beef or turkey
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F(190°C)
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, sauté the minced garlic and diced onion until fragrant and translucent
  3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned
  4. Drain the excess fat and stir in the marinara sauce, letting the flavors meld for a few minutes
  5. Prepare your cheese mixture by combining the ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella, Parmesan chees, beaten eggs, and Italian seasoning in a bowl
  6. Now it's time to assemble your lasagna. Begin by spreading a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a greased baking dish.
  7. Layer lasagna noodles, followed by a generous portion of the cheese mixture and more meat sauce. Repeat the layers until your dish is filled, finishing with a final layer of meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving, and enjoy.
